The goal of the game is to go as high as possible, collect money and get upgrades to get higher scores.
Collect fuel to continue flying and avoid obstacles.

Controls:
W - go forward
A - turn left
D - turn right
S - breaking
SPACE - activate speed boost.

Shop:
Various upgrades to improve acceleration, damage resistance, fuel consumption and more.

Sessions:
Previous 10 game sessions, listed by most recent.

Settings:
Color, rocket name and volume settings.
